The Best Movies According to Jes

My Top 3 Movies of all time!

Forrest Gump

Hands down my favorite movie of all time. Amazing story, amazing soundrack. Nothing compares.

The Breakfast Club

John Hughes directed some great movies, but this is probably his best. Hits you right in the feels. Also probably one of the best movies of the 80s.

Star Wars

I can't really narrow it down to just one Star Wars movie, but I suppose my favorite would have to be Revenge of the Sith. Entire series is incredible!
